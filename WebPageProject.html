Soon, he refined it more to intended Finance, but quickly made a jump into the STEM world with computer science. Most of Luke's second semester was in this major until switching into Early Childhood Education over the Summer. He finally settled on a major during add and drop week with MIS and a comajor in International Business.

At Webroot, he saw all sides of the technical world and realized although he wants to spend time in cyber-security, he does not want to spend 8 hours a day behind a computer. One of the most interesting parts of the company was the Security Analyst, which has the most direct pipeline through MIS.
